## Findings
- **Topics:**
    - Joint Venture (JV) Opportunities: High interest in splitting deals and collaborating.
    - Market-Specific Networking: Locating buyers and "boots on the ground" in cities like Kansas City, Dallas, and Houston.
    - Systems & Tool Stacks: Choosing between PropStream, BatchLeads, Podio, and REsimpli.
    - Lead Generation: Cold calling strategies, SMS delivery issues, and skip tracing.
    - Vacant Land: Increasing interest in wholesaling land vs. houses.
- **Pain points:**
    - High rejection rates and low pickup rates during cold calling.
    - Technical difficulties with SMS spam filters and "AI roadblocks."
    - "Shiny object syndrome" – being overwhelmed by too many tools and data providers.
    - Difficulty in verifying "serious" cash buyers.
- **Questions:**
    - "Who is new to wholesale?" (Networking/Icebreaker)
    - "What is the best lean CRM for solo operators?"
    - "Who has buyers in [specific city]?"
    - "How to avoid SMS spam filters?"
- **Sentiment:**
    - Energetic, hustle-focused, and highly collaborative.
    - Supportive of beginners ("FreeWholesaling.com" mission).
    - Transactional but community-driven.
- **Key Members:**
    - **Zach Ginn:** Primary leader and course creator.
    - **Anthony Orya:** Highly active in facilitating JV networking.
    - **Zachery Sherwood:** Active in local networking (Missouri area).
    - **Rick Ginn:** Co-founder/Influencer.
